    Oral care products are defined as products used for care of the teeth and the mouth.
    Various products are available based on their special clinical indications including toothpastes, mouthwashes, tooth-whitening products, and denture care materials.
    Although these products have gone through standard safety keep bacteria under control.
    However, without proper oral hygiene, bacteria can reach levels that might lead to oral infections, such as tooth decay and gum disease
